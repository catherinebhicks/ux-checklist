Competitive Analysis

Understanding the competitive landscape to inform design decisions

Duration 2-4 days
Difficulty Beginner
Phase Research

Overview

Competitive analysis is a critical first step in the UX design process. It involves systematically analyzing competitors' products, services, and user experiences to identify opportunities, understand industry standards, and avoid reinventing the wheel.

Why It Matters

  • Identifies market opportunities and gaps
  • Helps establish design patterns and conventions
  • Informs feature prioritization decisions
  • Prevents costly design mistakes
  • Provides benchmarks for success metrics
